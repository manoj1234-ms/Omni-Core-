## 🏗️ Phase 1: Deep Verification & "The Why"
Instead of a simple score, we provide **Explainable Confidence**.
- **Source Ranking Engine:** Assign trust weights to domains (e.g., `.gov`, `.edu`, `arxiv.org` = 0.95 | `randomblog.com` = 0.3).
- **Explanation Block:** Hub returns a `reason` field explaining the logic behind the grounding.
- **Deterministic Checks:** Add RegEx/Rule-based validators for dates, numbers, and specific tech specs (solving Problem 1 of v3.1).

## 📈 Phase 2: Adaptive Swarm Trust
Agents now have a "Cognitive Credit Score."
- **Performance Tracking:** Use Supabase to track an agent's historical `truth_score`.
- **Sliding Consensus:** Weighted voting will favor agents with higher historical accuracy.
- **Trust Decay:** Inactive or consistently wrong agents will have their `trust_score` decayed automatically.

## 🧬 Phase 3: The Knowledge Graph (Memory Evolution)
Moving from flat logs to linked intelligence.
- **Relationship Mapping:** Store memories as `Subject -> Relation -> Object` triplets.
- **Context Recency:** Prioritize newer verified facts in the Reality Matrix while archiving old ones.
